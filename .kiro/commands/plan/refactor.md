## Arguments
MODULE: $1 (required, module or area to refactor)

## Workflow
1. Analyze current implementation and pain points
2. Define refactoring goals and constraints
3. Identify affected tests and dependents
4. Plan incremental steps (each independently deployable)
5. Assess risk and define rollback strategy
6. Create plan document with task breakdown
